March 14, 2025 - 10:00 AM

Monitor Oil Collection Drum Usage

Security Team, an oil collection drum is located outside the recycling room in the loading dock area behind the building. It is designated for residents to dispose of cooking oil/grease to prevent clogs and floods in the building’s plumbing system.

Please monitor the area to ensure residents are using the drum correctly and report any misuse or overflow to management. Unauthorized disposal in kitchen sinks can lead to significant plumbing issues, so your vigilance is critical.

March 14, 2025 - 10:00 AM

Clarify Maintenance Responsibilities for Residents

Security Team, please note that residents are responsible for the maintenance and repair of all items within their units, including fixtures and systems that serve only their unit or an adjacent one (e.g., plumbing, electrical, appliances, and personal fixtures).

The building management handles maintenance of the fan-coil units. If residents report issues related to their personal fixtures, direct them to handle repairs themselves or contact the appropriate service provider. Your role is to ensure clarity on this division of responsibility during interactions with residents.

March 14, 2025 - 10:00 AM

Enforce Visitor Notification Protocol

Security Team, to maintain safety and manage traffic flow, ensure that residents notify the Gatehouse of all expected visitors. Your team must enforce this protocol by verifying visitor logs and ensuring all visitors are pre-authorized by residents.

Failure to follow this protocol can lead to unauthorized access and increased security risks. Please maintain strict oversight of visitor arrivals and report any issues to management immediately.

March 14, 2025 - 10:00 AM
